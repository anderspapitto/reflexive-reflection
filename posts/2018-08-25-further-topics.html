Wrap-up and Further Topics

There is a lot to say about Pseudopolis beyond the minimal core.
August 25, 2018 (pseudopolis)

That about covers the very core idea. Building an entire city and providing it as a service can be approached as a viable business.

There are many many further things to address (and I intend to) but they all build on that core. These further topics mostly fall into these broad categories:

  • Practical details of building and maintaining Pseudopolis, in terms of both the physical city and its people.
  • Elaboration on the social and economic factors that make the environment ripe for Pseudopolis.
  • Discussion of how to adapt Pseudopolis to be not only profitable but also a public good, the first city in the world which guarantees accessibility to all people.

Thanks for reading! If you’ve made it this far, please do message me to let me know what you found interesting and any thoughts you’ve had in response.
